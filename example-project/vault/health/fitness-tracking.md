# Fitness tracking

A simple, sustainable weekly routine that fits around freelance work and a
kitchen renovation.

## The week

| Day      | Session                                |
|----------|----------------------------------------|
| Mon      | Bodyweight strength A (push focus)     |
| Tue      | Cycle commute / errand ride (45-60 min) |
| Wed      | Bodyweight strength B (pull / core)    |
| Thu      | Rest or short walk                     |
| Fri      | Cycle (30-45 min)                      |
| Sat      | Long ride (60-120 min)                 |
| Sun      | Rest, mobility, garden                 |

## Bodyweight strength

Three rounds, no rush:

- Push-ups (work toward a clean set of 20)
- Inverted rows or pull-ups
- Squats (slow tempo, 15-20 reps)
- Plank (60-90s)
- Side plank, both sides

## Tracking

I record sessions in a single text file in `~/Documents/health/`. Just date,
session type, and a one-line note. Anything more elaborate gets abandoned
within a month — past attempts confirm this.

## Goals (low-key)

- Hit 3 strength sessions a week 80% of weeks across a quarter.
- Sub-3-hour ride at conversational pace by end of June.
- No injury list.
