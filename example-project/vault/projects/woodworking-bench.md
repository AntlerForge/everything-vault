# Joiner's bench build

A proper hardwood workbench for the back-garden workshop. Replaces the
cobbled-together folding-and-MDF rig I've used since I started.

## Design

- **Top:** laminated beech, 80mm thick, 1800 × 600.
- **Base:** trestle-style frame in Douglas fir, mortise-and-tenon joints.
- **Vises:** front vise (twin-screw) plus a tail vise. 19mm dog-hole pattern.
- **Tool tray:** a shallow tray at the back, removable.

Rough plans drawn up with reference to two well-known classic workbench
designs; specifics tweaked for my space (3.4m max length).
