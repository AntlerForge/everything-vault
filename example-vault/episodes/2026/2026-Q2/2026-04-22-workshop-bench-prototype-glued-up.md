# Bench top glued up

Two halves, four boards each. Clamped overnight, off the clamps the next
morning.

One half came out with a slight cup — about 1.5mm across the width. Less
than I feared. Built a quick router sled from offcuts and a length of
extruded aluminium left over from a previous build, and started flattening
both halves.

Final glue-up of the two halves into the full top scheduled for 2026-04-29.
